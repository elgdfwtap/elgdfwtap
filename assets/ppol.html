Electric Vehicle Charging Survey Privacy Policy

Purpose of the Survey

This Google Form is being used solely for the purpose of collecting responses for a class presentation. The information gathered will be used only for academic purposes related to that presentation.

Data Collection

This form does not collect email addresses or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily include such information in your responses.

Data Use

All responses will be reviewed and analyzed only for the purpose of the class presentation. The data will not be shared, published, or distributed to any third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

All responses will be deleted permanently after the completion of the class presentation. No copies or backups of the data will be retained.

Voluntary Participation

Participation in this survey is completely voluntary. You may choose not to participate or skip any question at any time.

Contact

If you have questions about this survey or how your information is handled, please contact erikliu2@illinois.edu.
