Entry 7.1

The site is clear, with no materials placed adjacent to the building. Two cones and two barriers are positioned along the track. Approximately 45 feet of additional wall is being prepared to install the wall. A section of the building is covered with plastic sheeting, indicating that recently applied materials are being protected from environmental exposure. There are around 20 crew members present on site. Two telehandlers are actively operating, one along the bottom path and the other on the side path. while three boom lifts are positioned along the left side of the building. One of these boom lifts is actively working on the second floor near the back. The blue telehandler is initially seen holding a bagged, white circular object while waiting on the left side of the site. It pauses while the orange telehandler transitions from the bottom path to the vertical path, which takes about one minute. Once the orange telehandler clears the way, the blue telehandler proceeds down the bottom path and successfully drops off its load.

Entry 7.2

At 1:07 PM, the blue telehandler drove to the middle of the left side of the building. It then returned to the material pickup area, where it loaded a container onto its forks. The telehandler completed the cycle by driving back to the same location at 1:15 PM, making the total material transport cycle approximately 8 minutes.

Entry 7.4

At 1:17 PM, a white truck pulled out from the first floor, shortly before the blue telehandler reversed and remained idle nearby. At 1:18 PM, the orange telehandler traveled down the vertical path and arrived at the material pickup area. It unloaded the container it was carrying, reloaded it, and then returned at 1:26 PM. Completing the loading and unloading cycle took approximately 8 minutes. After the orange telehandler passed, the blue telehandler proceeded to the material pickup area. It hooked and lifted a bag-shaped item, raised it, and transferred it to a higher level near the pickup zone. The telehandler then drove off at 1:29 PM.
